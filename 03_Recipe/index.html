Binangkal Recipe

A step-by-step guide to making Binangkal, a popular Filipino snack.

What is Binangkal

Binangkal is a popular snack in the Philippines, especially in the Visayas and Mindanao regions. These are deep-fried sesame-coated dough balls, crunchy on the outside and soft inside often paired with coffee or hot chocolate!

Try making it yourself!

Start

Step 1: Prepare Ingredients

Gather the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 medium eggs
  • 1/2 cup evaporated milk (or regular milk)
  • 1/2 cup sesame seeds (for coating)
  • 2 cups vegetable oil (for deep frying)
← →

Step 2: Mix Dry Ingredients

In a bowl, combine flour, sugar, and baking powder. Mix well.

← →

Step 3: Add Wet Ingredients

Add eggs and milk to the dry mixture. Stir until dough forms.

← →

Step 4: Shape and Coat

Shape the dough into small balls and coat with sesame seeds.

← →

Step 5: Fry Until Golden Brown

Heat oil in a pan and deep-fry until golden brown. Drain excess oil.

← →

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy

Let the Binangkal cool, then serve and enjoy with coffee!
